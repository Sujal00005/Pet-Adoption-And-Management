### 2️⃣ Create Favicon (5 minutes)
**Why:** Professional sites have a favicon (icon in browser tab)

**How to do it:**
1. Go to [Favicon.io](https://favicon.io/favicon-generator/)
2. Settings:
   - **Text:** P (or 🐾)
   - **Background:** #e8834a
   - **Font:** Nunito
   - **Font Color:** #ffffff
3. Click "Download"
4. Extract and upload these to your server in `static/images/`:
   - `favicon-32x32.png`
   - `favicon-16x16.png`
   - `apple-touch-icon.png`

---

### 3️⃣ Create Social Share Image (15 minutes)
**Why:** When people share your site, it'll look professional

**How to do it:**
1. Go to [Canva](https://canva.com) (free account)
2. Create custom size: 1200 x 630 pixels
3. Design:
   - Add cute pet photos
   - Add text: "Paws & Hearts 🐾"
   - Add tagline: "Find Your Perfect Companion"
   - Use orange color scheme (#e8834a)
4. Download as JPG
5. Save as `og-image.jpg` in `static/images/`

**Canva Template Ideas:**
- Search "Facebook Post" templates
- Use pet-themed templates
- Keep it simple and friendly
